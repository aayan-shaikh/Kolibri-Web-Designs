Kolibri Web Designs, We Make Websites With Purpose & Intent

My name is Aayan, I am the owner and lead developer of Kolibri Web Designs, a web design and development agency built with my partner. I got into programming before University. I wanted a career that I can do from home and still be travelling around the world. So over the course of one year I taught myself how to code websites the right way by hand. What I found was that a lot of small businesses don't have the funds to spend thousands of dollars upfront on a new website. And the ones that did, got taken advantage of and have a terrible website that looks like it was built by someone in a cave and haven't seen design trends for the last 10 years. They were in a frustrating spot - they either can't afford a good website, and if they can they don't know who to trust to make something great. So we have a business model to fit their needs.
